Difficult Obstacles Reducing Closed Sales

These are the hidden blockers that quietly derail sales momentum — not mistakes or a lack of effort, just familiar daily patterns where progress slows, confidence dips, or clarity vanishes right when it's needed most. The creatures merely put a face to the issue. The goal isn't to pretend they don't exist. It's to recognize who they are — and know what beats them. Select a suspect to open the case file.
