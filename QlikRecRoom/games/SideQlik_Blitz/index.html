HOW TO PLAY
THE BASICS

Aim and fire. Move your mouse to aim, click or press SPACE to launch a data orb at the D.O.R.C.S.

Match colors. Land your orb against 2 or more D.O.R.C.S. of the same color to knock the cluster down.

Hold the line. Clear them before the swarm descends past the bottom marker. Bigger clusters score bigger.

Earn new colors. Beat your territory's high score in a single run and a tougher D.O.R.C. color joins the board — proof you're competing at the top of your region.

POWER-UPS · DEFENSE
BRAIN FREEZE
Pop a question orb on the board and answer a Qlik product question. Get it right to slow the swarm's descent for a few seconds and buy yourself time.
BRAIN BLAST
Knock down D.O.R.C.S. to fill the Blast meter in your HUD. When it reads READY, click it to take on 3 Qlik glossary terms — nail all three to wipe the bottom rows.
POWER-UPS · METHODOLOGY
METHODOLOGY COIN
Pop a coin to answer a Korn Ferry methodology question. Bank 3 coins to trigger Methodology Madness.
METHODOLOGY MADNESS
Spin the wheel for 1–5 Madness Orbs — colorless bombs that vaporize every D.O.R.C. they touch and chain through their color.
